LEASEMASTER

Experience hassle-free tenant management with our intuitive solution.

Our Tenant Management System is a powerful and user-friendly platform designed to streamline and simplify the management of your rental properties. Whether you own a single property or manage a portfolio of rental units, our system provides you with the tools and features you need to efficiently handle all aspects of tenant management.

Tenant Screening

Conducting background checks on potential tenants, including credit history, criminal records, employment verification, and rental history.

Lease Management

Facilitates the creation, tracking, and management of lease agreements and allows landlords to generate standardized lease templates, customize terms and conditions.

Rent Collection and Accounting

Enables automated rent collection processes, including generating invoices, tracking payments, and sending rent reminders.

Maintenance and Repair Requests

Provides a platform for tenants to submit maintenance and repair requests online while allowing landlords to track and manage these requests.

Communication and Documentation

Offers communication tools to facilitate seamless communication between landlords and tenants.

Online Tenant Portal

Provides an online portal for tenants, allowing them to access important information and perform various tasks conveniently.
